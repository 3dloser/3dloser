In a galaxy far, far away, there exists a planet named Glorpglorp. Its residents, Glorpglorpians, feed exclusively on Marmite imported from Earth. Unlike humans, Glorpglorpians' spoon-shaped hands allow them to dig into the jars and eat Marmite by the spoonful.

That's probably why they run out of supply so quickly.
