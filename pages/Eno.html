Two posters / 2022

Graphic Design

About

As an attempt to translate a piece of music into a visual form, I folded and photographed a piece of paper to the tune of one of my favorite pieces of music, Brian Eno's Golden Hour. Then I designed this pair of posters with the photographs. Mr. Eno, I love you!!
