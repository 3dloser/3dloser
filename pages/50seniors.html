50 Seniors in 50 Nights


After Senior Week was cancelled during COVID-19, NYU Class Activities Board came up with the 50 Seniors in 50 Nights initiative to highlight seniors and celebrate their time at NYU. Any senior at NYU was encouraged to apply, and each day a senior was selected and featured on NYU Class of 2020's Instagram and Facebook page. I designed the logo for the campaign and created a template for each featured senior.
