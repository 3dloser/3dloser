Respecc Wamen: An Exhibition


This is an exhibition consisting of over 200 respect women memes.

As a Reddit user sums it up, this meme is an ironically condescending approach to an otherwise serious, genuine message."Respecting women" is a meme that started as a wholesome movement, which, like every other meme of the wholesome meme genre, is laced with a dose of satire. The reiteration of a painfully obvious objective has metastasized into a meta version of itself, and that is incredibly fascinating to me.
