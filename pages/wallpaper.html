Shitposting in the Age of Digital Reproduction

This is a wallpaper I made composed of images I found off of 4Chan's /pol/ board. These are words, images, and memes that have been reappropriated by the alt-right, or random internet people pretending to be the alt-right. The distinct drawing style is taken from Tumblr, where everything can be transformed into the pastel-grunge-teen-girl aesthetic. These two seemingly complete opposite corners of the Internet intersect in a strange, unsettling way.
