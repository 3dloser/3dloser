Rockefeller Center Flag Project


My illustration was selected to be flown on the Rockerfeller Center Plaza as part of the Flag Project from Aug 1-16, 2020. The Flag Project was a campaign to celebrate NYC during the pandemic, and this lil flag encompasses the NYC that I know and love. I would have loved to have a pic of the flag flying in its full glory in the sky, but let's just say...the wind conditions during summer in NYC are really not ideal.
