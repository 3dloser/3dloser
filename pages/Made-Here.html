Made Here / 2023

Book Design

About

​A best cookbook of 2023 chosen by Bon Appetit, Vogue, Forbes, ​Food Network, ​The LA Times, and more, this cookbook in collaboration with Send Chinatown Love is a collection of recipes, photos, and in-depth stories from 43 restaurants featuring 18+ Cuisines in 24 NYC neighborhoods.

As the art director and lead designer of this special project, I worked closely with creative director Nat Belkov to design this cookbook from scratch. I learned so much in the process of making this book and am so grateful for the team of incredible people I got to work with.

​100% of net proceeds of this cookbook goes back to funding Send Chinatown Love’s community-building efforts.
