About

I interned at Instabot in 2018 where I had to learn Illustrator in one day (a story for another time!); by the end of the internship I learned how to use After Effects, and these were the first animated GIFs I ever made. Just a couple of bots inside their very real chat bot office, doing cute little bot things like drinking bottled water during meetings and pressing some beep boop buttons.
