Race to Moonchella

Click here to enter the game!

This is a game I coded with Kat Vlasova and Junyang Ma using the P5 framework. I designed the sets, characters as well as the overall interface.

In this game, you control characters such as Father John Misty and Lil Pump, and your goal is to help them get dressed while dodging various obstacles so they can make it to Coachella 2020 on the moon (sponsered by Elon Musk)(Coachella 2020, not this game)(Although Elon if you are reading this I guess it would be cool if you sponser us thanks).
