Race to Moonchella / 2018

Game Design

About

Race to Moonchella is a game I coded with Kat Vlasova and Junyang Ma using the P5.js framework. I designed the sets, characters as well as the overall interface.

In this game, you control characters such as Father John Misty and Lil Pump, and your goal is to help them get dressed while dodging various obstacles so they can make it to Coachella 2020 on the moon.
