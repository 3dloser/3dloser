Birds of Aotearoa / 2023

Illustration & Silkscreen Prints

About

A series of designed and silkscreened t-shirts focused on the history of two Aotearoa birds: the Takahē and the (now extinct) Huia, each combined with the landscapes of the habitats they reside(d) in.

Printed label cards were attached to each shirt to explain the history behind the birds and objects on the shirts.

All profits were donated to the Takahē Recovery Programme and Forest & Bird New Zealand.
