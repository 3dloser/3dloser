NYU Senior Week


NYU Senior Week is a yearly tradition that consists of a week of events and ceremonies to celebrate the graduating class. Even though I was a junior at the time, I was hired by NYU to create designs for the 2019 Senior Week. This year was the first year that NYU decided to incorporated a theme, for which we chose the iconic NYC pizza rat (in space). I created all illustrations and designs, which included Facebook ads, Instagram posts, printed materials on posters, merch, and more.
