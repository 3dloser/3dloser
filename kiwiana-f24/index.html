"I am re-creating an idealised New Zealand childhood. I wanted a 1950s place, and to fill it with 1950s things. When I go there it is like acting out a fantasy about family holidays there, decades ago. It’s like I’ve bought myself a national back-story or auto-biography in material form. Whatever happens in the future, it is my own little world, even though it is modelled on myths about another world, 1950s New Zealand."

Text excerpt from sociologist Dr Claudia Bell's interview with a kiwiana collector.

Kiwiana index

1 Four Square: New Zealand's first supermarket and chain

2 Watties: New Zealand based packaged food company, famous for its slogan: "You'll always be a Kiwi, if you love our Wattie's Sauce"

3 Buzzy bee: wooden toy that became popular in New Zealand during the post-war baby boom

4 Hokey Pokey ice cream: vanilla ice cream with lumps of honeycomb toffee

5 Edmonds: most known for its baking powder, has been described as synonymous with kiwi home cooking and baking

6 Gumboots: often worn by farmers when sheep shearing and conducting agricultural activities

7 L&P soft drink and meat pies: a popular food combo
