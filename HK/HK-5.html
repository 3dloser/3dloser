At the height of the Hong Kong protests, I decided to check out what people were saying on Weibo (Chinese Twitter). The results were hard to watch--people defending police brutality, calling the protestors cockroaches, decades of anger pouring out onto the pages, each post garnering tens of thousands of likes and supportive comments. There was no discourse, no room for consideration, just pure, unadultered hatred.

I guess I saw this coming, but still, witnessing it right before my eyes made me feel sick. My stomache twisted into knots, and I felt desperately helpless. There is no reasoning with a monolith.

5/8
